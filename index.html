National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Islamabad Campus

FAST-NUCES Islamabad, also known as the Foundation for Advancement of Science and Technology - National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, is a leading institution of higher education in Pakistan. Established in 2000, FAST-NUCES is renowned for its focus on technology and management education. The university offers a range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as Computer Science, Software Engineering, Business Administration, and Electrical Engineering.
